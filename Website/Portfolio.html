Behold my portfolio

Over the years but especially recently I have been creating projects inside and outside of university. They vary from level design to 2D art, 3D modelling and web development. Beneath you can find an oversight of these projects. By hovering over and clicking on a project, you can find out more information about the project and my involvement in it.

Portfolio items

Project Year 1: Burn The Mob

With a team of 6 other highly motivated peers, I worked two weeks on a physics based game. The game is a spin on the idea of burning a witch at the stake. Instead you, as the witch, can use your abilities to defend yourself from the mob. Read More

Project Year 1: Slope Madness

Slope Madness is the result of a two week project. In the end we had an actual board people could stand on and tilt to steer. The goal is to go down a mountain, avoid obstacles such as trees and other fallen snowboarders. Read More

Assignment Year 1: Maya Treehouse

For an assignment in my first year of college we had to model a 3D treehouse. I decided to make a model based on the fairy tale of Jack and the Beanstalk. I first made the big beanstalks by extruding a vertex and adding skin and subdivision to it, thus giving it the tickness it needed. Read More

Project Year 1: Shyrdna

The first project I made in an engine with a team ended up being The Downfall of Shyrdna. It's a point and click game in which the player plays as a girl in a dystopian world who has lost her parents. She must solve puzzles to save her sister, who has fallen sick. Read More

Multiple 3D models
