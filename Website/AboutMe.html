About me

Take a look at information about me and my skills below

Hi! I’m Julie. Currently I’m studying Creative Media and Game Technologies in The Netherlands. Since a young age, I've liked to be working creatively and I was always trying to figure out how things worked. As I grew up, I found I had a passion for organizing and leading creative projects, willing to help where needed and always up for a challenge. I'm good at taking initiative and I am able to quickly pick up new skills.

I enjoy developing websites, 3D modeling (in Blender) and sketching in my free time. I try to develop myself as an all-round designer that people can rely on for work from level design and game scripting to narrative design and planning. I enjoy organizing a project, making sure people know what to do and then ensuring the project is finished in time for a deadline.
